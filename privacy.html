At The Snippet, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our student community. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a secure environment, we collect the following information:

  • Verification Data: Your college-issued email ID (e.g., @college.edu.in) used to verify your student status.
  • Profile Information: Your real name, gender, and college name for your Primary Profile.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, and operating system for security and app performance.
  • Location Data: Approximate location for the Map-based event discovery feature.

2. Dual Profile & Anonymity

Privacy Note: Your Lowkey Profile uses a randomly generated alias. While other users cannot see your real name when you post via Lowkey, this profile is internally linked to your Primary Profile to prevent abuse and comply with Indian law.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify that you are a genuine student.
  • To facilitate the Student Marketplace and Event Discovery.
  • To maintain safety and moderate content on the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations under the IT Act, 2000.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your verification details and profile information are stored securely. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

5. Disclosure of Data

We may disclose your personal information (including the identity behind a Lowkey Profile) only under the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirement: If required by law, court order, or government request (e.g., under IT Rules, 2021).
  • Safety: To protect the safety of our users or the public.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your account and associated personal data. You can exercise these rights through the app settings or by contacting our Grievance Officer listed in the Terms of Service.

7. Third-Party Services

The Platform may use third-party services like map providers or analytics tools. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.
